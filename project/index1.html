Emergency Room Plateform

innovation to help you get better care

This is a new plateform to help you with saving time in the Emergency Room. Fill in this form and make sure you data is accurate before walking in to the emergency room

Please skip this form and walk-in directly into the emergency room if you are feeling lightheaded, with severe palpitations, severe shortness of breath or suspected fractured bones

This is made shorten the waiting time for cold cases where there is no further need to take personal information is the form was filled. The information entered will be saved and only used with re-entry of the data again
